Hatch Eggs Macro

This macro will hatch eggs while your pets grind the best zone.

Features:

  • Drop flags in the best zone (preferences defined in Settings.ini).
  • Drop sprinklers in the best zone (preferences defined in Settings.ini).
  • Hatch your best egg.
  • Eat multiples of fruit as often as you want (defined in Settings.ini).
  • Claim your free gifts.
  • Use your ultimate in the best zone.
  • Auto-Reconnect.

Configuration:

  • README.txt - Contains more information about the macro, and all the requirements to get it running.
  • Settings\Settings.ini - Contains all the user settings.
  • Advanced configuration can be performed in the following AHK files:
    • Modules\Coords.ahk - All of the coordinate settings.
    • Modules\Delays.ahk - All of the delays between actions.
    • Modules\Movement.ahk - All of the movement paths.
