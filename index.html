About Us

DEAL Clan is a thriving community within the world of Pet Simulator 99! We pride ourselves on our camaraderie and dedication to helping each other grow stronger. With 28 members and counting, we're steadily climbing the ranks and making our mark in the game.

Membership Requirements:

  • Attendance to all clan events and quests is mandatory
  • Monthly gem donations are required to support clan growth
