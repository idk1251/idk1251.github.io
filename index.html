About Us

Welcome to DEAL Clan in Pet Simulator 99! We're a strong community dedicated to helping each other grow and succeed in the game.

With 28 active members and counting, we're looking for dedicated players who want to be part of something bigger.

Our clan motto is: "Together We Are Strong! Help Each Other!"

Zirjagor is the proud owner of the clan, leading us to greatness.

Rules & Requirements

  • Must be rank 10+ in Pet Simulator 99.
  • Attendance to all clan events and quests is mandatory.
  • Monthly gem donations are required to support the clan's growth.
  • Inactive members or those who don't contribute will be swiftly kicked from the clan.
  • To become an officer, demonstrate dedication through significant gem donations or clan quest contributions.
